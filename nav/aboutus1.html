Dedicated to the conservation of wildlife and their habitats. Our mission is to inspire and educate visitors about the natural world through engaging experiences and interactive exhibits.

Our History

Established in 1927, the Columbus Zoo has grown to become one of the most renowned zoos in the world. With a commitment to animal welfare, conservation, and education, we host over 2 million visitors annually and support numerous conservation projects globally.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead and inspire by connecting people and wildlife. We strive to create memorable experiences that foster a deeper understanding and appreciation for the natural world.

Conservation Efforts

At Columbus Zoo, conservation is at the heart of everything we do. We actively participate in and support numerous conservation projects worldwide, focusing on protecting endangered species and preserving their natural habitats.

Community Engagement

We believe in giving back to our community. Through educational programs, outreach initiatives, and special events, we aim to engage and inspire the next generation of wildlife enthusiasts and conservationists.
