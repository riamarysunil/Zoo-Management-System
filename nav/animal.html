ANIMALS

All Mammals Birds Reptiles Aquatic Amphibians Insects

African Lion

The African lion is known for its majestic appearance and strength. These lions live in prides and are primarily found in sub-Saharan Africa.

Asian Lion

The Asian lion, smaller and shyer than its African counterpart, resides in the Gir Forest of India. Conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.

Bengal Tiger

The Bengal tiger is the most numerous tiger subspecies. Known for their power and beauty, they are found in India, Bangladesh, Bhutan, and Nepal.

Siberian Tiger

The Siberian tiger, the largest tiger species, roams the forests of eastern Russia. They are renowned for their strength and resilience in harsh climates.

Bald Eagle

The bald eagle, a symbol of strength and freedom, is native to North America. They are known for their powerful build and keen eyesight.

Scarlet Macaw

The scarlet macaw, with its vibrant plumage, is a standout in the tropical forests of Central and South America. They are highly social and intelligent birds.

Nile Crocodile

The Nile crocodile, found throughout sub-Saharan Africa, is a formidable predator. Known for their size and strength, they inhabit freshwater environments.

King Cobra

The king cobra, the world's longest venomous snake, is found in forests from India to Southeast Asia. They are revered and feared for their potent venom.

Blue Whale

The blue whale, the largest animal ever known to have lived, roams the world's oceans. They are gentle giants that primarily feed on krill.

Clownfish

Clownfish, made famous by the movie Finding Nemo, live among sea anemones in warm ocean waters. They are known for their bright colors and unique relationship with anemones.

Red-Eyed Tree Frog

The red-eyed tree frog, with its striking red eyes and green body, is a nocturnal inhabitant of Central American rainforests. They are known for their vivid appearance.

Axolotl

The axolotl, a unique amphibian native to Mexico, is known for its ability to regenerate limbs. They are critically endangered in the wild.

Monarch Butterfly

The monarch butterfly is renowned for its long migration from North America to central Mexico. They are a symbol of transformation and endurance.

Praying Mantis

The praying mantis, named for its prayer-like stance, is a master of camouflage and an adept predator of insects. They are found in temperate and tropical habitats worldwide.
